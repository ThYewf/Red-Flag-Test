We're a team of cybersecurity students passionate about making the internet safer for everyone.

Our Goal

To educate teenagers (13–18) about online predators, grooming tactics, and how to stay safe online. We want to empower you to spot the red flags and protect yourself in the digital world.

What We Offer

  • Fake Giveaway Simulation – Experience a real-world scam scenario and learn how to avoid it.
  • Interactive Posters – Learn visually with our custom-designed safety posters.
  • Red Flag Quiz – Test your knowledge and see if you can spot the warning signs.

Who We Are

We're cybersecurity students using real-world strategies and the latest research to raise awareness and help you stay safe online. Our mission is to make digital safety accessible, practical, and engaging for teens.

Awareness is your best defense.

Stay alert. Stay smart. Stay safe online!
